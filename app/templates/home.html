About

This project was inspired by the need for effective virtual collaboration and study environments, especially in the digital age where remote learning has become crucial.Our goal was to create a platform that allows students to connect, share resources, and collaborate in real-time. This idea emerged from personal experiences with the challenges of remote studying.
